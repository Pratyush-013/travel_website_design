What is included in a typical adventure package?

Our packages typically include expert guides, accommodation, meals, necessary equipment, and transportation during the trip. Specific inclusions vary by adventure.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellation policies vary depending on the tour and time of cancellation. Please refer to the specific tour details or contact our support team for more information.

Do I need prior experience for these adventures?

Many of our adventures are suitable for beginners, while others require a certain level of fitness or experience. Each tour description specifies the required skill level.
